      ! [conflicted] x found in 3 packages.
      Either pick the one you want with `::`:
      * a::x
      * b::x
      * c::x
      Or declare a preference with `conflict_prefer()`:
      * `conflicts_prefer(a::x)`
      * `conflicts_prefer(b::x)`
      * `conflicts_prefer(c::x)`
